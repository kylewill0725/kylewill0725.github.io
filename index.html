Isolated

This is for if you want your Warframe windows seperated from the rest of your chrome windows.

  1. Make a new shortcut to chrome on your desktop.

  1. Open properties of shortcut.

  1. Append --profile-directory=AutoTwitch to the Target text box.

  1. Press apply.
  2. Run the shortcut, open this page in the new window, and install the extension.
  1. Go to https://www.twitch.tv and login.
  1. Load a stream, set the volume to zero, and the quality to as low as possible.

Running

  1. Open a new window. Use the custom shortcut if you did the isolated section.
  2. Go to:
      3. https://kylewill0725.github.io/blank.html?start
  3. The extension icon should turn blue when running. When done, just close the window.
